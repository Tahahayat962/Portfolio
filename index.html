BScene website

Redesigned a website based on user research, for a client.
The issue:
Clients Wiktor Kulinski and Jason Freeze came to us with a problem: The BScene website was not as visible as they wanted it to be, and wanted us to conduct user research, find out what was wrong with it, and come up with a solution.
The process:
First, we started with some user research. We conducted 20 interviews in total with a diverse range of demographics to get the general idea of what people thought about Brant region and its culture, and additionally BScene as well. Then, we came up with 2 personas: The local lover, and the local hater.
The prototype:
Based off of these personas, we created some wireframes on InDesign, and then a mostly interactive working prototype on Figma. We then conducted usability testing with 10 people, mostly of the older demographics, to test its user-friendliness.
